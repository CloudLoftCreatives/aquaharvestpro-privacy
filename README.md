# aquaharvestpro-privacy
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025
Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Aqua Harvest Pro (“we”, “our”, “us”) is an aquaculture productivity application designed to help users calculate harvest weights, manage tray data, generate reports, and store harvest records locally on their device. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the limited information handled by the app.

By using the Aqua Harvest Pro application (“App”), you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Aqua Harvest Pro does not collect, request, or store any personal or sensitive user information, including but not limited to:

Name

Email address

Phone number

Login credentials

Contacts

Photos or media

Location data

Device identifiers

The App does not require user accounts or sign-in.

2. Data Stored on Your Device (Local Storage Only)

All data created in Aqua Harvest Pro is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

Harvest calculations

Tray weight settings (single tray / double tray)

Saved harvest records

Generated PDF reports

History and archive data

App preferences and custom configurations

This data is not transmitted to our servers and is not accessible to us.

If you uninstall the App or clear app data, locally stored information may be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

3. Permissions Used by the App

Aqua Harvest Pro may request the following permissions:

Storage Access (If Applicable)

Used only to save and export PDF reports to your device

Used to allow you to share generated files with others

Notification Permission (Optional)

Used to provide optional reminders or app-related alerts

The App works normally even if notifications are disabled

No other permissions are requested.

4. PDF Generation and Sharing

When you generate or share PDF reports:

Files are created locally on your device

Sharing is initiated only by the user

We do not receive, store, or access any shared files

5. Data Security

Because Aqua Harvest Pro does not collect or transmit personal data, privacy risks are minimal. We use reasonable technical safeguards to ensure that locally stored data remains secure within the App environment.

6. Third-Party Services

Aqua Harvest Pro does not use third-party advertising services, tracking SDKs, or analytics platforms that collect personal information.

If future updates introduce optional analytics or crash reporting, they will be limited to anonymous technical data and this policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Aqua Harvest Pro is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

8. Your Choices

You may:

Customize tray weights and app settings at any time

Export, share, or delete saved data manually

Disable notifications through device settings

Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect feature updates or regulatory requirements. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” above.
